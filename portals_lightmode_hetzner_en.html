FAQ

How can I download my Hetzner invoices?

With GetMyInvoices you connect your Hetzner account once; after that your invoices are downloaded automatically. You set the retrieval interval yourself, and documents already captured are not downloaded twice.

Are my Hetzner login details safe with GetMyInvoices?

Your login details are stored encrypted and cannot be viewed by GetMyInvoices staff. All data is transmitted over HTTPS and hosted in Germany. GetMyInvoices is also GDPR-compliant.

What happens if the Hetzner login fails or something changes?

If a login fails, you receive a notification and can update your login details. Automatic retrieval then continues as normal.

Can I connect several Hetzner accounts?

Yes, you can connect several accounts of this portal separately. The receipts from all accounts come together centrally in GetMyInvoices.

Where can I export my Hetzner invoices?

GetMyInvoices supports over 50 integrations, including DATEV, Lexware Office and sevdesk. You set the export schedule independently of the retrieval interval.
